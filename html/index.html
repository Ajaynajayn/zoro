  • Enhancing Safety and Ergonomics

    Incorporating return conveyor systems promotes a safer and more ergonomic work environment.

  • Increasing Efficiency

    Return conveyor systems simplify the material handling process in edge banding machines. They automatically guide finished panels back to the starting point, eliminating the need for manual interventions.

  • Minimizing Waste

    Return conveyor systems ensure accurate and consistent positioning of panels, contributing to minimal material waste. Panels are precisely guided, allowing for precise machining of the edges without errors or defects.

  • Optimizing Space and Resource Utilization

    Unlike manual material handling, which can require larger storage areas, return conveyor systems streamline material flow and make optimal use of limited space.

  • Facilitating Productivity and Competitiveness

    The integration of return conveyor systems boosts overall productivity and enhances manufacturers' competitiveness in the market.

The Genesis of Our Journey

AMS Technologies is a small-scale enterprise specializing in the manufacturing of CNC (Computer Numerical Control) machines and high-precision industrial components. The company is dedicated to providing high-quality machinery parts that cater to various milling and industrial processes. Its product range includes mill blades, rice mill Jali, rice mill shafts, and other essential machine components. AMS Technologies is committed to delivering innovative solutions in machine manufacturing, ensuring efficiency, durability, and precision in every product. With a focus on small and medium-scale manufacturers, the company strives to enhance the production capabilities of its clients through its advanced machining techniques.

With a primary focus on small and medium-scale manufacturers, AMS Technologies helps businesses optimize their production capabilities. By providing reliable and cost-effective spare parts, the company supports industries that require high-precision machinery for milling, agricultural processing, and other industrial applications. AMS Technologies is committed to sustainable manufacturing practices by minimizing material waste and improving energy efficiency in production. The company aims to expand its product range and enhance its technological capabilities to better serve its growing customer base.
